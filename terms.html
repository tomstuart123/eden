TSS - Toxic Shock Syndrome

TSS is a rare, but very serious condition. It is a complication of bacterial infections, which has been associated with the use of tampons. However, it is not restricted to women or the use of tampons. In order to get TSS, a person needs to have a specific strain of staph bacteria (staphylococcus aureus) in their body. It is possible to contract TSS when using a cup although it is extremely rare. There have only been a few cases of TSS linked to the use of menstrual cups. To reduce the risk, we recommend only using the cup for maximum 12 hours at the time and take it out to clean it, make sure to wash your hands before inserting and removing the cup. We, Eden Cup, cannot be held liable for any cases of TSS linked to the use of the cup.

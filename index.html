YOUR EDEN CUP

The EDEN CUP is a 100% FDA approved silicon menstrual cup designed and ready for whatever your period throws at you. Eden Cup was created by our founder Georgina and specifically designed to adapt to your body's natural shape to form a secure and leak-free seal. Combined with our no waste ethos and our 100% recyclable packaging. You have no reason to ever use tampons or pads again!

BENEFITS OF AN EDEN CUP

FOR YOUR BODY:

Comfort: The flexible, silicone adapts to your body making it comfortable and easy to wear

Convenience: "You can wear your Eden Cup for up to 10 hours. It doesn't matter whether you're running, swimming or sleeping"

Hygenic: The Eden Cup is made from medical grade silicone, and is FDA approved. It contains no chemicals often found in tampons and pads.

FOR THE PLANET:

Zero Waste Product: Eden Cups last a lifetime, the equivalent of over 10,000 tampons.

Carbon Footprint: Our providers have to adhere to the highest ethical standards as our team always look to reduce our footprint

Zero waste Packaging: We use recyclable cardboard and our paper instructions are printed on eco-friendly paper to prevent additional waste. The cotton storage bag is unbleached organic cotton and has been certified as a 'Bag of Ethics'

HOW DOES IT WORK?

It's super simple. Just follow the steps below to get started with your Eden Cup:

1. Boil in a saucepan for 5-7 minutes. Allow to cool.

2. Squeeze, fold and insert the cup

3. Wear for up to 10 hours

4. Remove, empty, wash and reuse

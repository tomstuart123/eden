ABOUT EDEN

We begun Eden as we want Period Care to get better! We want the environment to get better! And we aren't going to stay quiet about it!

Our first product - the EDEN CUP - is a menstrual cup designed around you! We believe the more people who switch to menstrual cups, the more the planet and womenkind benefit. Win-Win!

THE EDEN CUP

The EDEN CUP is designed from product to packaging to make switching from traditional period care a more comfortable, convenient and easier experience!

BENEFITS OF EDEN

We focus on making Eden Cups the best Period care solution:

Comfort: Bendable and large curved design makes the Eden Cup even more comfy

Hygenic: EdenCup is 100% Silicon, FDA approved and has zero chemicals

Convenience: You can use Eden Cup for up to 12 hours. Even if you empty it away from home, just empty, wipe and re-insert

Switching to Eden cups also helps do your bit for the planet:

Lower Waste: 1 Eden Cup can last over a year... i.e. over 500 tampons or 99% less wastage

Packaging: Buy through the site and we deliver with our own custom packaging to limit waste

Give to Charity: We put our money where our mouth is. For every cup you buy, we donate to our local UK wildlife trust. We care about our countryside and want to keep it the way it is

HOW DOES IT WORK?

It's super simple. Just follow the below steps to get started with your Eden Cup:

1. Squeeze, fold and Insert the Cup

2. Wear it for up to 12 hours

3. Remove the Cup

4. Empty, wash and reuse

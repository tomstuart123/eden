ABOUT EDEN

We launched Eden as we want to give women environmental choices for their period care! The EDEN CUP is a menstrual cups specifically designed to be comfortable, leak-free and environmentally friendly!

We believe the more people who switch to menstrual cups, the more the planet and womenkind benefit. So join us for a cleaner, more convenient and cheaper period!

THE EDEN CUP

The EDEN CUP is a 100% silicon menstrual cup designed and ready for whatever your period throws at you. Eden Cup was created by our founder Georgina to be flexible and adapt to your body's natural shape to form a secure and leak-free seal. Combined with our no waste ethos and our 100% recyclable packaging, you have no reason to ever use tampons or pads again!

BENEFITS OF EDEN

We focus on making Eden Cups the best Period care solution:

Comfort: The flexible, silicone adapts to your body making it comfortable and easy to wear

Convenience: You can wear your Eden Cup for up to 10 hours without worry

Hygenic: The Eden Cup is made from medical grade silicone, and is FDA approved. It contains no chemicals often found in tampons and pads.

Switching to Eden cups also helps do your bit for the planet:

Zero Waste Product: Eden Cups last a lifetime, the equivalent of over 10,000 tampons.

Zero Waste Packaging: All our packaging materials and the handy cup storage bag are 100% recyclable.

Carbon Footprint: We look beyond materials to our other operations to try and limit our wider impact on planet earth

HOW DOES IT WORK?

It's super simple. Just follow the below steps to get started with your Eden Cup:

1. Boil in a saucepan for 5-7minutes. Allow to cool.

2. Squeeze, fold and insert the cup

3. Wear for up to 10 hours

4. Remove, empty, wash and reuse
